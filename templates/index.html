AI Settings

When is AI used?
Only in the Analyze iFrame tab — and only when the iframe URL doesn’t match a known platform (YouTube, Vimeo, Google Maps, etc.). The AI reads the page’s title, description, and headings to generate a WCAG-appropriate title suggestion.
Embed Checker and Scan Page are 100% rule-based and never use AI.

Use this if the iframe src is a relative path or you only have a URL.

Why title attributes matter

WCAG 2.2 SC 4.1.2 and Technique H64 require all <iframe> elements to have a meaningful title attribute. Screen reader users hear this title before deciding whether to enter the frame.
